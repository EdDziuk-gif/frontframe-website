business today?

Most business owners couldn't. Not because the business isn't valuable — but because nothing is documented, transferable, or owned cleanly enough to hand to someone else.

The website lives in a platform they don't control. The design decisions exist only in someone's memory. The AI assistant, if there is one, was configured by a vendor who holds the keys.

When a buyer asks what they're getting, the answer is: access. Not ownership.

The FrontFrame Standard

When you build with FrontFrame,
