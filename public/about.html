Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 12, 2026

FrontFrame LLC respects your privacy. This policy describes how we handle communications related to our website.

SMS Notifications

FrontFrame's website uses an AI chat assistant to help visitors explore our services. When a visitor signals readiness to speak with a person, an automated SMS alert is sent to the business owner only. No visitor phone numbers are collected, stored, or transmitted. The sole recipient of these notifications is the FrontFrame business owner.

No Customer Data Collected

FrontFrame does not collect, store, or share visitor phone numbers. No marketing messages are sent. No opt-in is required from website visitors.

Visitor Contact Requests

When a visitor voluntarily submits contact information through the website to request a conversation, that information is used solely to facilitate the requested contact. It is not stored, sold, or used for any other purpose.

Client Communications

Clients engaged in an active project may receive SMS messages related to their engagement, including requests to review deliverables or respond to time-sensitive questions. These messages are transactional in nature and directly related to the services being performed under the client agreement. Consent to receive these messages is captured via initialed acknowledgment in the service agreement executed prior to engagement. Clients may opt out at any time by replying STOP.
