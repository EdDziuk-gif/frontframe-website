## What We Do (One-paragraph summary)

FrontFrame builds and sells turnkey business platforms for small service businesses and solo professionals. It is not a web design studio. Each engagement delivers a complete, ready-to-launch business foundation — including some or all of: a professional public-facing website, intake forms, e-signature workflow, payment integration, client and admin portal, and operational workflow structure.

## Visual Identity Direction

**Concept:** Architectural/blueprint aesthetic. The anchor graphic is a front elevation line drawing with the word FRONT labeling the left half and FRAME labeling the right half, separated by a dashed vertical line. A door straddles the dividing line. A window sits upper left. Diagonal hatching fills the elevation.

**Color direction:** Blueprint blue (primary accent), structural gray (neutral), off-white or pale blue (background), one warm accent, near-black (body text). Specific hex values to be confirmed when designer delivers palette.

**Typography direction:** Geometric or transitional sans-serif for headings. Neutral sans or readable serif for body. Specific typeface selection delegated to designer.

**What to avoid:** Stock photography, gradient-heavy SaaS aesthetics, device mockup photography, anything resembling a web design portfolio or agency website. Photography reserved for client case study sections only.
